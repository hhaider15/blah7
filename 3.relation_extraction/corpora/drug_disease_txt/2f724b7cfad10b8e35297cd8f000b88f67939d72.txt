0|chunk|First Complete Genome Sequence of a Feline Alphacoronavirus 1 Strain from Brazil

1|chunk|We identified a strain of Alphacoronavirus 1, FCoV-SB22, from a pool of fecal samples from domestic cats from a rural settlement in the municipality of Santa Brbara, Par, Brazil. The nucleotide identity with feline coronavirus was 91.5%. The present study reports the first complete genome sequence of a feline coronavirus from Brazil.
