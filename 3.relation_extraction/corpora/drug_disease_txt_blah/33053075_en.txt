0|chunk|Functional development of swallowing in ICU patients with COVID-19.

1|chunk|to describe de functional development of swallowing in Intensive Care Unit (ICU) patients with COVID-19, who were submitted to a swallowing intervention.participants of the study were 77 patients (both gender, mean age 53.415.9; score on the Glasgow Coma Scale 14 and stable respiratory condition). The functional scale of swallowing used for assessment was the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association National Outcome Measurement System (ASHA NOMS).the results indicate that there was a significant recovery of the functional swallowing patterns when comparing the measurements pre and post swallowing intervention.83% of the patients needed up to 3 swallowing interventions to recover a safe swallowing pattern.
