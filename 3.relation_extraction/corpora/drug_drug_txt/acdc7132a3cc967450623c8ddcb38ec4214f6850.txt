1|chunk|There is very limited data on the time trend of diabetes incidence in Asia. Using populationlevel data, we report the secular trend of the incidence of type 1 and type 2 diabetes in Hong Kong between 2002 and 2015.

2|chunk| Our results raised concerns over the escalating burden of young-onset diabetes in Asia.
