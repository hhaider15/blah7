0|chunk|Virological Surveillance of Influenza Viruses during the Seasons in Tunisia

1|chunk|Background: The data contribute to a better understanding of the circulation of influenza viruses especially in North-Africa.

2|chunk|Objective: The objective of this surveillance was to detect severe influenza cases, identify their epidemiological and virological characteristics and assess their impact on the healthcare system.

3|chunk|We describe in this report the findings of laboratory-based surveillance of human cases of influenza virus and other respiratory viruses' infection during three seasons in Tunisia.
