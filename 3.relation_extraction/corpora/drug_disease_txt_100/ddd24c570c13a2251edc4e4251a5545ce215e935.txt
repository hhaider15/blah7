0|chunk|Meeting report: the 4 th symposium on animal models of non-human primates in Kunming, Yunnan, China

1|chunk|Since its establishment in 2005, the KPRC has become a highly-regarded NHP research and development center, serving as one of the major bases for biomedical research in China.

2|chunk|As emphasized in the symposium, NHP animal models are not only irreplaceable in basic research, but also of critical importance in a wide range of scientific and clinical investigations. The symposium, together with the previous series (Yao et al., 2015) , also provided opportunities to promote communication and facilitate collaboration among researchers in the field of NHP studies, from basic research to translational medicine.
