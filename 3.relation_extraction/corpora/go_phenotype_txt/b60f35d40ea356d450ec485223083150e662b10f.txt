0|chunk|Complete Genome Sequence of Strain SDCV/USA/Illinois121/2014, a Porcine Deltacoronavirus from the United States

1|chunk|To investigate the causative agent of swine diarrhea, next-generation sequencing (NGS) was performed on a porcine fecal sample. The NGS reads were assembled, which generated a complete swine Deltacoronavirus genome sequence, that of strain SDCV/ USA/Illinois121/2014.
