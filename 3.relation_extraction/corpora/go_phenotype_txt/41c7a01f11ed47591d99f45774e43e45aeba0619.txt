0|chunk|BMC Microbiology CAPIH: A Web interface for comparative analyses and visualization of host-HIV protein-protein interactions

1|chunk|The Human Immunodeficiency Virus type one (HIV-1) is the major causing pathogen of the Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome (AIDS). A large number of HIV-1-related studies are based on three non-human model animals: chimpanzee, rhesus macaque, and mouse. However, the differences in host-HIV-1 interactions between human and these model organisms have remained unexplored.
