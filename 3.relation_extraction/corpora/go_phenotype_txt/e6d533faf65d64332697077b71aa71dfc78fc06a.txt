0|chunk|Environmental Health: A Global Access Science Source The effects of injection of bovine vaccine into a human digit: a case report

1|chunk|The incidence of needlestick injuries in farmers and veterinary surgeons is significant and the consequences of such an injection can be serious.

2|chunk|We report accidental injection of bovine vaccine into the base of the little finger. This resulted in increased pressure in the flexor sheath causing signs and symptoms of ischemia. Amputation of the digit was required despite repeated surgical debridement and decompression.

3|chunk|There have been previous reports of injection of oil-based vaccines into the human hand resulting in granulomatous inflammation or sterile abscess and causing morbidity and tissue loss.
