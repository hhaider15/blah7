0|chunk|Lyon-IARC Polyomavirus DNA in Feces of Diarrheic Cats

1|chunk|A viral metagenomic analysis of feces from an unexplained outbreak of feline diarrhea revealed the presence of Lyon-IARC polyomavirus (LIPyV) DNA. LIPyV, whose genome was originally sequenced from swabs of human skin, was fecally shed by three out of five diarrheic cats.
