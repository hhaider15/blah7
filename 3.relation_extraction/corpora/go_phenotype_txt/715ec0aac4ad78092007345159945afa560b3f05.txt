0|chunk|Strengthening the Detection of and Early Response to Public Health Emergencies: Lessons from the West African Ebola Epidemic Summary Points

1|chunk| The international response to the West African Ebola virus disease epidemic has exemplified the great potential of the global public health community. However, the protracted early response also revealed critical gaps, which likely resulted in exacerbation of the epidemic.

2|chunk| It is incumbent on international health partners to learn from missteps that occurred in the early stages of the epidemic and strengthen our public health capacity to better respond to future public health emergencies.
